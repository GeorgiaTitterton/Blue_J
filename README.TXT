------Game Shop-------

As you enter this shop start by entering the ammount of money you have in your account

Within the store you can purchase a range of different equipment to help you in games;
  -  Weapons ($20)
  -  Health ($15)
  -  Super Strength ($10)
  
Click on your account in the bottom bar and purchase whatever you'd like from the store

Once you are finished Click "Checkout" to see what you got and how much you spent


MAKE sure you keep track of your spending because you might spend too much

HAVE FUN!!!!!!
